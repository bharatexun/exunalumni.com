Delhi Mixer: 27th October 2024

TL;DR

    Why? To help old and new Exun alumni connect with each other.

  • Date: October 27th, 2024
  • Time: 3PM - 6PM
  • Venue: India International Centre, New Delhi
  • Cover: ₹1000

    • VPA: bharatkashyap.exun@okicici


    Register for Event

We're holding a mixer in Delhi on October 27th, 2024 - the first of its kind. The plan is for it to be a casual event with 20 people at the maximum, at a venue that's convenient for everyone.

The idea is to hold this as a "pilot" event - bring younger and older Exun alumni together, hold some light-hearted competitions (read quizzes) to break the ice, create a platform for them to share ideas and experiences, and see how we can help each other out in the future. Hopefully, the pilot event turns into a template for future events across cities.

The event will have a small registration fee of ₹1000, which will be used to cover the costs of the venue, and some food and drinks.

It's happening at the India International Centre, New Delhi. We're excited to see you there!
